New York Style Cheesecake

Prep Time Additional Time Total Time Servings Course
30 mins 1 hour cooking + 6 hours cooling 7 hours 30 mins 12 Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 15 graham crackers, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.

Step 2: Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a medium bowl until well combined.

Step 3: Press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust.

Step 4: cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Blend in milk, then mix in eggs, one at a time, until just combined.

Step 5: Add sour cream, flour, and vanilla; mix until smooth.

Step 6: Pour filling onto prepared crust. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.

Step 7: Turn the oven off; let cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours to prevent cracking. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.

Optional: If desired, garnish with assorted berries and whipped cream.

Gulab Jamun

Prep Time Additional Time Total Time Servings Course
20 mins 20 mins cooking + 3 hours resting 3 hours 40 mins 14 - 18 jamuns Dessert

Ingredients:

For Jamuns:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 5 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ghee or oil + 1 tbsp for greasing
  • 2 to 4 tbsp milk (use more as needed)
  • 1 tbsp curd(yogurt or ¾ tbsp lemon juice)
  • 1 large pinch of baking soda
  • ghee or oil for deep frying
  • 2 tsp pistachios chopped

For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 ¼ to 1 ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 pods green cardamom or ¼ tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp rose water

Making Jamuns

Step 1: Mix together flour, milk powder, and baking soda in a bowl.

Step 2: Add ghee and mix well.

Step 3: Add 1.5 tbsp of yogurt to the flour mixture and bring dough together. If it is too dry, add a little more milk or curd.

Step 4: The dough should be sticky. Grease your fingers with ghee and make a ball. The dough should hold the shape well and be smooth.

Step 5: Divide into 14 to 18 equal portions. When finished, keep covered.

Step 6: Heat up pan with ghee or oil on medium heat.

Step 7: To test if your oil is the right temperature, drop a small piece of dough in. It should rise slowly without changing color.

Step 8: When ghee or oil is medium hot add the balls. If it is too hot, the inside will not cook well.

Step 9: Fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Then, lower flame and fry until golden-brown. Keep stirring gently.

Preparation of Sugar Syrup

Step 1: Mix together water, sugar, and crushed cardamoms in a pot.

Step 2: Boil the syrup until it turns slightly sticky. It should still be fairly thin.

Step 3: Add rose water and mix. Keep hot.

Step 4: Leave jamuns to sit in sugar syrup for at least 3 hours.

Optional: Garnish with chopped pistachios.

Source: Gulab Jamun Recipe - Swasthi's Recipes

Butter Chicken

Prep Time Additional Time Total Time Servings Course
1 hour 15 mins At least 1 day marinating 25 hours 15 mins 5 Main

Ingredients:

Chicken Marinade

  • 1 lb. boneless chicken breast, cut into 1 ½ inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon EACH: ginger paste and garlic paste
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Murgh Makhani Curry

  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 ½ teaspoon EACH: ginger paste and garlic paste
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • ¼ teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves (crushed between fingers)

Step 1: In a medium bowl, combine the tandoori masala, ginger, garlic, and yogurt. Whisk until smooth, adjust seasonings to preference. Add the chicken and allow to marinate for at least 20 minutes and ideally for 12-24 hours, covered in the refrigerator.

Step 2: Heat the ghee in a dutch oven or heavy bottom pot over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until they turn translucent and start to sweat, about 5-7 minutes, don't allow the onions to brown. Add ginger and garlic paste and let cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn't burn. Add the can of crushed tomatoes along with the chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder and continue to cook for 5 minutes, if the mixture starts bubbling rapidly, add about ¼ cup of water and continue to cook.

Step 3: Remove from heat, add the mixture to a blender and blend until smooth, you may need to add a couple water to help it blend (up to ¼ cup). Depending on how powerful your blender is, you may want to blend the mixture in two batches. Remember to hold the lid of the blender down with a kitchen towel when blending, to avoid accidents.

Step 4: MAKE AHEAD OPTION: At this point you can cool and refrigerate the sauce for 24-48 hours (or freeze it for up to 3 months), it will help blend the flavors even more, just remember to bring the sauce back to room temperature (use the microwave) before proceeding with the recipe OR you can continue on with the recipe immediately.

Step 5: Heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in the dutch oven over medium heat. Add the marinated chicken (discard any excess marinade) and cook for about 5-6 minutes, stirring as required to brown all sides. Add the butter chicken sauce to the pot and heat everything through. Once it starts to bubble, add the cream and garam masala. When the sauce regains a simmer, add the crushed fenugreek leaves.

Step 6: Serve over basmati rice or with naan.

Source: Finger Lickin' Butter Chicken Recipe | Little Spice Jar
